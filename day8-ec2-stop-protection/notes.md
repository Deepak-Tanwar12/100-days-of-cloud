## Key Concepts

- Helps avoid unplanned downtime
- Commonly enabled on production or critical instances
- Acts as a safeguard against human error
- Part of operational and reliability best practices

## Why This Matters
In real-world environments, not all issues come from system failures.
Many outages happen due to accidental actions.

Stop protection helps:
- prevent accidental shutdowns
- protect critical workloads
- improve reliability
- enforce operational discipline
