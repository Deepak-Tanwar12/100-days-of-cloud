## Key Concepts

- Can be enabled or disabled anytime

## Practical Task

## Why This Matters
In real cloud environments, accidental termination can:
- cause downtime
- result in data loss
- interrupt services

Simple safeguards like termination protection help maintain reliability and operational safety.
